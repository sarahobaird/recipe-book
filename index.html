Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 1 hr
  • Serves: 24
Ingredients Quantity
Softened Butter 1 cup
Sugar 1 cup
Packed brown sugar 1 cup
Eggs 2
Vanilla extract 2 teaspoons
Baking soda 1 teaspoon
Hot water 2 teaspoons
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
All-purpose flour 3 cups
Semisweet Chocolate Chips 2 cups
Walnuts 1 cup

Step 1: Preaheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

Step 2:Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.

Step 3:Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.

Molasses Cookies

  • Time: 1 hr
  • Serves: 30
Ingredients Quantity
Melted Butter 3/4 cup
White sugar 1 cup
Eggs 1
Molasses 1/4 cup
All-purpose flour 2 cups
Baking soda 2 teaspoons
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Ground cinnamon 1 teaspoon
Ground cloves 1/2 teaspoon
Ground ginger 1/2 tablespoon
White sugar 1/2 cup

Step 1: In a medium bowl, mix together the melted margarine, 1 cup sugar, and egg until smooth. Stir in the molasses. Combine the flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, cloves, and ginger; blend into the molasses mixture. Cover, and chill dough for 1 hour.

Step 2: Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Roll dough into walnut sized balls, and roll them in the remaining white sugar. Place cookies 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets.

Step 3 Bake for 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven, until tops are cracked. Cool on wire racks.

Brownies

  • Time: 25 min.
  • Serves: 16
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 1/2 cup
White sugar 1 cup
Eggs 2
Vanilla extract 1 teaspoon
Unsweetened cocoa powder 1/3 cup
All-purpose flour 3 cups
Salt 1/4 teaspoon
Baking Powder 1/4 teaspoon
Frosting
Butter, softend 3 tablespoons
Unsweetend cocoa powder 3 tablespoons
Honey 1 tablespoon
Vanilla extract 1 teaspoon
Confectioners'Sugar 1 cup

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour an 8-inch square pan.

Step 2:In a large saucepan, melt 1/2 cup butter. Remove from heat, and stir in sugar, eggs, and 1 teaspoon vanilla. Beat in 1/3 cup cocoa, 1/2 cup flour, salt, and baking powder. Spread batter into prepared pan.

Step 3:Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes. Do not overcook.

Step 4:To Make Frosting: Combine 3 tablespoons softened butter, 3 tablespoons cocoa, honey, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, and 1 cup confectioners' sugar. Stir until smooth. Frost brownies while they are still warm.

Rice Crispy Treats

  • Time: 30 min.
  • Serves: 24
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 1/4 cup
Mini Marshmallows 4 cups
Rice Crispy Cereal 5 cups

Step 1:Melt butter in large sauce pan over low heat. Add marshmallows and stir until melted and well-blended. Cook 2 minutes longer, stirring constantly. Remove from heat.

Step 2:Add cereal. Stir until well coated.

Step 3:Using buttered spatula or waxed paper, press mixture evenly and firmly in buttered 13 x 9 inch pan. Cut into 2 x 2 inch squares when cool.
